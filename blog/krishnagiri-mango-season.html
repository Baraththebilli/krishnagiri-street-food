When is Krishnagiri Mango Season?

The Krishnagiri mango season runs from April to July every year. This is the best time to visit Krishnagiri if you want to enjoy fresh, juicy, and affordable mangoes straight from the orchards!

Why Krishnagiri is the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu

Krishnagiri district produces more mangoes than any other district in Tamil Nadu! The fertile soil, perfect climate, and generations of farming expertise make Krishnagiri mangoes truly special and unique!

Best Mango Varieties in Krishnagiri

  • 🥭 Alphonso — The king of mangoes, known for its rich sweetness
  • 🥭 Banganapalli — Large and juicy, perfect for eating fresh
  • 🥭 Neelam — Sweet and fragrant, great for juices
  • 🥭 Totapuri — Slightly tangy, perfect for pickles and chutneys
  • 🥭 Malgova — Large and fleshy, a local favourite in Krishnagiri

Where to Buy Mangoes in Krishnagiri

The best place to buy fresh Krishnagiri mangoes is at the local fruit markets near the main bus stand. You can also visit mango orchards directly during the season for the freshest mangoes at the best prices!

Mango Festival in Krishnagiri

Every year during mango season Krishnagiri hosts a spectacular Mango Festival! This festival celebrates the rich mango heritage of Krishnagiri with exhibitions, tastings, and competitions featuring hundreds of mango varieties!

Mango Street Food in Krishnagiri
