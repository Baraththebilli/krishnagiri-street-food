Top Restaurants in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri has a wonderful variety of restaurants ranging from local Tamil Nadu cuisine to North Indian and Chinese food. Here are the best restaurants in Krishnagiri that you must visit!

1. Hotel Sangeetha ⭐ 5.0

One of the highest rated restaurants in Krishnagiri! Hotel Sangeetha is located in Co-operative Colony and is known for its good and tasty food. A must visit for anyone looking for quality meals in Krishnagiri!

📍 11, Co-operative Colony Rd, Bairappa Colony, New Pet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 75020 94649

2. Sree Saravana Bhavan ⭐ 3.5

A popular vegetarian restaurant located on the Krishnagiri to Bangalore Highway! Sree Saravana Bhavan is open 24 hours and serves authentic South Indian vegetarian food. Perfect for travelers passing through Krishnagiri!

📍 Krishnagiri to Bangalore Highway, Krishnagiri

⏰ Open 24 hours

3. Hotel Mangalam Non Veg A/C ⭐ 4.0

One of the most popular non vegetarian restaurants in Krishnagiri with over 7000 reviews! Hotel Mangalam is famous for its outstanding mutton biryani made with seeraga samba rice and mutton nalli pepper fry. A must visit for biryani lovers in Krishnagiri!

📍 Near Fly Over, Rayakotta Road, Londenpet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 73390 97543

⏰ 8:30 AM to 10:30 PM daily

4. Hunger Brunch ⭐ 4.7

One of the highest rated cafes in Krishnagiri! Hunger Brunch is located on Salem Bypass Road and is famous for its delicious shawarma, chicken chilli momos, sandwiches, falooda and fresh juices. Budget friendly and perfect for friends and families!

📍 Salem Bypass Road, Thiruvalluvar Nagar, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 82488 50208

⏰ 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM daily

Budget Tips for Eating in Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Lunch time meals are cheaper than dinner
  • ✅ Local restaurants offer better value than tourist spots
  • ✅ Try the unlimited thali meals for best value
  • ✅ Street food is the cheapest and most authentic option
