Top Restaurants in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri has a wonderful variety of restaurants ranging from local Tamil Nadu cuisine to North Indian and Chinese food. Here are the best restaurants in Krishnagiri that you must visit!

1. Hotel Sangeetha

One of the most popular restaurants in Krishnagiri, Hotel Sangeetha is famous for its authentic Tamil Nadu cuisine. Their biryani and thali meals are absolutely delicious and very affordable!

2. Anjappar Chettinad Restaurant

For authentic Chettinad cuisine in Krishnagiri, Anjappar is the place to go! Known for their spicy and flavourful dishes, this restaurant is a favourite among locals and tourists alike!

3. Junior Kuppanna

Junior Kuppanna is famous across Tamil Nadu for their authentic Kongu cuisine. Their mutton biryani and variety rice dishes are a must try when visiting Krishnagiri!

4. Hotel Saravana Bhavan

For the best vegetarian food in Krishnagiri, Hotel Saravana Bhavan is the top choice! Their idli, dosa, and South Indian thali are famous across the district!

5. Royal Biryani House

If you are specifically looking for the best biryani in Krishnagiri then Royal Biryani House is a must visit! Their Krishnagiri style biryani is cooked fresh every day using traditional recipes!

Budget Tips for Eating in Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Lunch time meals are cheaper than dinner
  • ✅ Local restaurants offer better value than tourist spots
  • ✅ Try the unlimited thali meals for best value
  • ✅ Street food is the cheapest and most authentic option
