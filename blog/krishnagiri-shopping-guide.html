Shopping in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri offers a wonderful shopping experience for tourists and locals alike! From fresh mangoes and local spices to traditional Tamil Nadu handicrafts, there is something for everyone in the markets of Krishnagiri!

Best Things to Buy in Krishnagiri

  • 🥭 Fresh Krishnagiri Mangoes — The most popular souvenir!
  • 🌶️ Local Spices — Fresh and aromatic spices at very affordable prices
  • 🍯 Natural Honey — Pure forest honey from local farmers
  • 🎨 Handicrafts — Traditional Tamil Nadu handicrafts and pottery
  • 👗 Traditional Textiles — Beautiful silk and cotton fabrics
  • 🥜 Local Snacks — Homemade snacks and sweets to take home

Best Markets in Krishnagiri

Main Market Road

The Main Market Road in Krishnagiri town is the best place for everyday shopping. You will find fresh vegetables, fruits, spices, and local products at very affordable prices here!

Mango Market

During mango season the Mango Market in Krishnagiri is a must visit! Hundreds of mango varieties from local farmers are available here at the best prices. A paradise for mango lovers!

Weekly Shandy Market

The weekly shandy market in Krishnagiri is a traditional open air market where local farmers and vendors sell fresh produce, handicrafts, and local products. A true authentic Tamil Nadu experience!

Shopping Tips for Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Bargaining is accepted at local markets
  • ✅ Morning is the best time to shop for fresh produce
  • ✅ Carry cash as most local shops don't accept cards
  • ✅ Buy mangoes directly from farmers for best prices
  • ✅ Visit the weekly shandy for the most authentic experience
