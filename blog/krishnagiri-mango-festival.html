What is the Krishnagiri Mango Festival?

The Krishnagiri Mango Festival is one of the most celebrated annual events in Tamil Nadu! Held every year during the peak mango season, this festival celebrates Krishnagiri's rich mango heritage and showcases hundreds of mango varieties from local farmers and orchards!

When is the Krishnagiri Mango Festival 2026?

The 32nd Krishnagiri Mango Exhibition (Mangani Thiruvizha) 2026 began on July 10, 2026 and is currently ongoing! Don't miss this amazing celebration of Krishnagiri's famous mangoes!

Where is the Krishnagiri Mango Festival 2026 Held?

The 32nd Krishnagiri Mango Exhibition 2026 is being held at the prestigious Government Arts College Ground, Krishnagiri! The venue is easily accessible from all parts of Krishnagiri and nearby cities like Bangalore and Hosur!

What to Expect at the Krishnagiri Mango Festival

  • 🥭 **Mango Exhibition** — Hundreds of mango varieties on display
  • 🏆 **Mango Competition** — Best mango variety contest
  • 🍹 **Mango Tasting** — Try different mango varieties for free
  • 🛍️ **Mango Market** — Buy fresh mangoes directly from farmers
  • 🎭 **Cultural Programs** — Traditional Tamil Nadu performances
  • 🍚 **Food Stalls** — Amazing local street food including biryani!

Popular Mango Varieties at Krishnagiri Mango Festival

  • 🥭 Alphonso — The king of mangoes
  • 🥭 Banganapalli — Large and juicy
  • 🥭 Neelam — Sweet and fragrant
  • 🥭 Totapuri — Slightly tangy
  • 🥭 Malgova — Large and fleshy
  • 🥭 Imam Pasand — Rare and delicious
