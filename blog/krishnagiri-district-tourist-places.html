Tourist Places in Krishnagiri District

Krishnagiri district is full of amazing tourist attractions! From beautiful parks and dams to ancient temples and stunning waterfalls — the district has something for every type of traveler! Here is your complete guide to all tourist places across Krishnagiri district!

Parks and Dams in Krishnagiri District

1. KRP Dam Park ⭐ 4.1

One of the most popular tourist attractions in Krishnagiri district with over 5000 visitors! KRP Dam Park offers stunning sunset views, peaceful green surroundings and a beautiful reservoir. Fresh fish is also available from local vendors near the dam!

📍 Bellarapalli, Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 9:00 AM to 6:30 PM daily

💡 Best time to visit is evening for beautiful sunset views!

2. Krishnagiri Dam Falls ⭐ 4.4

The Krishnagiri Dam Falls near KRP Dam is a beautiful natural attraction! The clean water and scenic surroundings make it a perfect spot for photography and picnics. Best visited between September and February after the monsoon season!

📍 KRP Dam, Bellarapalli, Tamil Nadu

💡 Best visited September to February for best water flow!

Famous Temples in Krishnagiri District

3. Sri Kalabairava Swamy Temple ⭐ 4.8

One of the highest rated temples in Krishnagiri district! Sri Kalabairava Swamy Temple is known for its divine energy, beautiful architecture and the incredible free food (annadanam) served to all visitors throughout the day! Located in the foothills of three sacred mountains!

📍 Kandikuppam, Palepalle, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 8:00 AM to 9:30 PM daily

💡 Free meals served all day — a truly spiritual experience!

4. Shiva Temple Doddametrai ⭐ 4.4

A hidden gem in Krishnagiri district! The Shiva Temple Doddametrai is uniquely situated in the middle of the Ponnaiyar river — making it one of the most unique temples in Tamil Nadu! About 60 km from Bangalore, this is a perfect weekend getaway!

📍 Ayaranapalli, Tamil Nadu

💡 Perfect for nature lovers, photographers and spiritual seekers!

5. Sri LakshmiNarasimha Swamy Temple ⭐ 4.6

An ancient and serene temple located on the Krishnagiri-Vellore road. The Sri LakshmiNarasimha Swamy Temple is known for its peaceful atmosphere, positive energy and beautiful surroundings. A must visit for devotees and spiritual travelers!

📍 Varatanapalli, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 6:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily

Waterfalls in Krishnagiri District

6. Bikkanapalli Falls ⭐ 4.2

A scenic hidden waterfall nestled among remote villages in the hills of Krishnagiri district! Best visited during monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak. The surrounding greenery makes it a beautiful spot for nature lovers and photographers!

📍 Bikkanapalli, Hosapalli, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM daily

💡 Best visited during monsoon season — July to October!

7. Perandapalli Forest Waterfalls ⭐ 4.0

An untouched natural waterfall hidden in the forests of Krishnagiri district! Perfect for raw nature lovers who want to escape the city and enjoy peaceful surroundings. Ideal for picnics with family and friends!

📍 Gobasandiram, Tamil Nadu

💡 Carry your own food and water — limited facilities available!

8. Kothakuriki Waterfalls ⭐ 4.1

A beautiful waterfall that looks just like Kerala with its coconut trees and flowing water! Located near Shoolagiri in Krishnagiri district, Kothakuriki Waterfalls is a perfect hidden gem for a peaceful sunrise or sunset experience!

📍 Shoolagiri, Ayaranapalli, Tamil Nadu

⏰ Open 24 hours

💡 Best visited at sunrise for a truly magical experience!

Tips for Visiting Tourist Places in Krishnagiri District

  • ✅ Carry enough water and food for remote locations
  • ✅ Visit waterfalls during monsoon season for best experience
  • ✅ Start early morning to avoid heat and crowds
  • ✅ Two wheelers are best for remote waterfall locations
  • ✅ Check weather conditions before visiting waterfalls
  • ✅ Respect nature — don't litter!
