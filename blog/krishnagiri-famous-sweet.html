Famous Sweets in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri has a wonderful tradition of making delicious sweets and desserts! From the famous Krishnagiri Halwa to traditional Tamil Nadu sweets, the sweet shops in Krishnagiri are a paradise for sweet lovers! Here is your complete guide to the most famous sweets in Krishnagiri!

1. Krishnagiri Halwa 🍩

The most famous sweet in Krishnagiri is the legendary Krishnagiri Halwa! Made from wheat flour, sugar, ghee, and cardamom, this rich and delicious sweet has been a Krishnagiri specialty for generations. The halwa has a unique texture and flavour that makes it different from any other halwa in Tamil Nadu!

✅ Available at most sweet shops in Krishnagiri town

💰 Price: ₹150 to ₹300 per kg

2. Mysore Pak 🍬

Due to Krishnagiri's proximity to Karnataka, Mysore Pak is a very popular sweet in Krishnagiri! Made from gram flour, ghee, and sugar, this rich and crumbly sweet is available at most sweet shops in Krishnagiri!

✅ Available at sweet shops across Krishnagiri

💰 Price: ₹200 to ₹400 per kg

3. Palkova 🥛

Palkova is a traditional Tamil Nadu milk sweet that is very popular in Krishnagiri! Made from slowly reduced milk and sugar, palkova has a rich creamy texture and delicious taste. It makes a perfect gift to take home from Krishnagiri!

✅ Available at dairy shops and sweet stalls

💰 Price: ₹200 to ₹350 per kg

4. Mango Sweets 🥭

During mango season Krishnagiri has amazing mango sweets that are unique to the district!

  • 🥭 Mango Halwa — rich and fragrant
  • 🥭 Mango Burfi — sweet and aromatic
  • 🥭 Mango Ladoo — traditional mango sweet ball
  • 🥭 Mango Ice Cream — fresh and delicious

✅ Available during April to July mango season

5. Payasam 🍮

Payasam is a traditional Tamil Nadu sweet dish that is very popular in Krishnagiri! Made from rice or vermicelli cooked in milk and sugar with cardamom and dry fruits, payasam is served at festivals, temples, and special occasions across Krishnagiri!

✅ Available at restaurants and sweet shops

💰 Price: ₹30 to ₹80 per serving

6. Ladoo 🟡

Various types of ladoo are very popular in Krishnagiri! From besan ladoo to motichoor ladoo to rava ladoo, these round sweet balls are available at most sweet shops in Krishnagiri and make perfect gifts!

✅ Available at sweet shops across Krishnagiri

💰 Price: ₹150 to ₹300 per kg
