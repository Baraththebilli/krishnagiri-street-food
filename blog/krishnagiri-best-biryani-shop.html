Best Biryani Shops in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is famous for its incredible biryani! The district has many amazing biryani shops that serve some of the most delicious biryani in Tamil Nadu. Here is your complete guide to the best biryani shops in Krishnagiri!

1. Noor Hotel ⭐ 4.2

One of the most loved biryani shops in Krishnagiri! Noor Hotel is located on Stadium Road and is famous for its amazing taste and value for money biryani. Their chicken biryani is particularly popular among locals and tourists!

📍 Stadium Road, Wahab Nagar, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 95974 23290

2. Aasife Biryani ⭐ 4.2

A very popular biryani shop in Krishnagiri located near the Rayakottai Flyover! Aasife Biryani is known for its aromatic biryani with perfectly balanced spices, tender meat and generous portions. Open daily from 11 AM to 11 PM!

📍 Near Rayakottai Flyover, Anna Arch, Somarpettai, Londenpet, Krishnagiri

⏰ 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily

3. Hotel Mangalam ⭐ 4.0

One of the most popular biryani shops in Krishnagiri with over 7000 reviews! Hotel Mangalam is famous for its outstanding mutton biryani made with seeraga samba rice. A must visit for biryani lovers in Krishnagiri!

📍 Near Fly Over, Rayakotta Road, Londenpet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 73390 97543

⏰ 8:30 AM to 10:30 PM daily

4. Sibaa Restaurants ⭐ 4.0

A hidden gem in Krishnagiri! Sibaa Restaurants is famous for its mutton biryani with generous portions of tender and juicy mutton pieces. Located on Salem Main Road, this is a must visit for biryani lovers!

📍 Salem Main Road, New Pet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 94435 29696

⏰ 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily

5. Salem RR Biryani ⭐ 3.7

Salem RR Biryani is a popular highway restaurant located just 2 km before Krishnagiri Toll Plaza on the Chennai-Bangalore Bypass! Famous for its Chicken Bucket Biryani, Tandoori Chicken and Fried Rice. A popular stop for highway travelers passing through Krishnagiri!

📍 Chennai-Bangalore Bypass, Bayanapalli, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 10:00 AM to 10:30 PM daily

💡 Good parking space available — perfect for highway travelers!

What Makes Krishnagiri Biryani Special?

  • ✅ Special blend of local spices unique to Krishnagiri
  • ✅ Traditional dum cooking method
  • ✅ Use of seeraga samba rice for extra aroma
  • ✅ Fresh and locally sourced ingredients
  • ✅ Generous portions at affordable prices

Tips for Eating Biryani in Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Visit during lunch 12pm to 3pm for freshest biryani
  • ✅ Popular shops sell out quickly — arrive early!
  • ✅ Try mutton biryani for the most authentic experience
  • ✅ Ask for extra raita and salna on the side
