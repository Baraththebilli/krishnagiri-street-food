What Makes Krishnagiri Biryani Special?

Krishnagiri biryani is unique because of its special blend of local spices and the traditional dum cooking method. The result is a fragrant, flavourful biryani that is unlike any other biryani in Tamil Nadu!

Ingredients for Krishnagiri Biryani

  • 🍚 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 🍗 500g chicken
  • 🧅 3 large onions sliced
  • 🍅 2 tomatoes chopped
  • 🧄 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 🌶️ 3 green chillies
  • 🥛 1 cup yogurt
  • 🌿 Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • ⭐ Whole spices — bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon
  • 🧂 Salt to taste
  • 🫒 4 tablespoons oil or ghee

Step by Step Krishnagiri Biryani Recipe

Step 1 — Prepare the Rice

Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Then boil the rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain and keep aside.

Step 2 — Marinate the Chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, green chillies, and salt. Marinate for at least 30 minutes for best results!

Step 3 — Cook the Masala

Heat oil in a large pan. Fry onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 15 minutes on medium heat.

Step 4 — Layer the Biryani

Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken masala. Add fresh mint and coriander leaves between the layers for that authentic Krishnagiri biryani flavour!

Step 5 — Dum Cooking

Cover the pan tightly with a lid and cook on very low heat for 20-25 minutes. This dum cooking method is the secret to authentic Krishnagiri biryani!

Step 6 — Serve

Gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita and boiled eggs. Your authentic Krishnagiri biryani is ready to enjoy!

Tips for Perfect Krishnagiri Biryani

  • ✅ Always use good quality Basmati rice
  • ✅ Don't skip the marination step
  • ✅ Low heat dum cooking is the secret!
  • ✅ Fresh mint leaves make a big difference
  • ✅ Use ghee instead of oil for richer flavour
