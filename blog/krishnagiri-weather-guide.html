Krishnagiri Weather Overview

Krishnagiri has a tropical climate with hot summers, a pleasant monsoon season, and cool winters. The weather in Krishnagiri is generally warm throughout the year making it a great destination to visit any time!

Summer in Krishnagiri (March to June)

Summers in Krishnagiri are hot with temperatures ranging from 30°C to 40°C. This is also the famous mango season so despite the heat it is a great time to visit for mango lovers! Stay hydrated and enjoy fresh mango juice from the local street stalls!

Monsoon in Krishnagiri (July to September)

The monsoon season brings heavy rainfall to Krishnagiri making the landscape green and beautiful. Temperatures drop to a comfortable 25°C to 30°C during this period. The Krishnagiri Dam looks stunning during the monsoon season!

Winter in Krishnagiri (October to February)

Winter is the best time to visit Krishnagiri! Temperatures range from 18°C to 28°C making it cool and very pleasant. This is perfect weather for sightseeing, trekking to Krishnagiri Fort, and exploring local food!

Best Time to Visit Krishnagiri

  • 🌸 October to February — Best for sightseeing and outdoor activities
  • 🥭 April to July — Best for mango season and mango festival
  • 🌧️ July to September — Best for nature lovers and dam views

What to Wear in Krishnagiri

  • ☀️ Summer — Light cotton clothes and sunscreen
  • 🌧️ Monsoon — Light raincoat or umbrella
  • ❄️ Winter — Light jacket for evenings
