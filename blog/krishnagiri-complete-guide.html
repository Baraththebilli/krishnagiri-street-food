Welcome to Krishnagiri!

Krishnagiri is a beautiful and vibrant district in Tamil Nadu, India! Known as the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu, Krishnagiri is famous for its delicious mangoes, amazing street food, historic forts, and stunning natural beauty! This complete guide covers everything you need to know about Krishnagiri!

About Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri district is located in the northwestern part of Tamil Nadu, bordering Karnataka. The district headquarters is Krishnagiri town which is situated on NH44, the main highway connecting Chennai and Bangalore. With a rich history and vibrant culture, Krishnagiri is one of the most interesting districts in Tamil Nadu!
