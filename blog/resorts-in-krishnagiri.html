Best Resorts Near Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is home to some excellent resorts, perfect for a family getaway, a weekend escape, or even hosting a corporate event! Here is our complete guide to the best resorts near Krishnagiri.

1. Trishvam Symphony Luxury Resorts & Spa ⭐ 4.8

The most premium resort near Krishnagiri! Set at the foothills of the Krishnadevaraya Hills, Trishvam Symphony offers pool villas, a spa, an amphitheatre, mango orchards, and a dedicated conference hall — making it perfect for both leisure stays and corporate retreats.

📍 Mallasandiram, near Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu

📞 +91 1800 258 3100

2. JF Resorts (Jain Farms Resorts)

An eco-friendly farm resort spread across 750 acres in Belathur! JF Resorts offers a peaceful, back-to-nature stay with a wholesome vegetarian restaurant, plantation tours, bullock cart rides, and a family-friendly atmosphere — great for a quiet weekend away from the city.

📍 Bagalur, Malur Road, Belathur Post, Krishnagiri District

3. CK Holidays - The Rural Heaven Resort

A relaxed rural-themed resort in Agaram with an outdoor pool, on-site restaurant, and comfortable rooms with private patios — a solid choice for a simple, comfortable getaway near Krishnagiri.

📍 Behind Sivasakthi Department Store, Agaram, Krishnagiri

4. Ashokaa Blue Green Resort

A well-rated resort in Karimangalam, popular with both couples and families looking for a getaway close to Krishnagiri.

📍 Ramapuram, Karimangalam, near BCR School, Krishnagiri

5. Fortuna Hosur

A popular resort and event venue in Hosur, built for large gatherings! Fortuna Hosur features a Grand Ball Room that seats up to 1000 guests and an open-air Grand Garden for up to 1500 — making it a great choice for weddings, corporate events, and large celebrations near Krishnagiri.

📍 Krishnagiri Main Road, Hosur
