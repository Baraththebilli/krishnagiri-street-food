What is Krishnagiri Famous For?

Krishnagiri is a beautiful and vibrant district in Tamil Nadu that is famous for many things! From its world class mangoes to its unique biryani, historic forts and stunning waterfalls — Krishnagiri has a rich heritage and culture that makes it truly special! Here is your complete guide to everything Krishnagiri is famous for!

1. Krishnagiri is Famous for Mangoes 🥭

The most famous thing about Krishnagiri is its mangoes! Krishnagiri is known as the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu and produces more mangoes than any other district in the state! The Krishnagiri mango season runs from April to July and attracts visitors from across India!

2. Krishnagiri is Famous for Biryani 🍚

Krishnagiri biryani is one of the most famous dishes in Tamil Nadu! Made with seeraga samba rice, special local spices and tender meat using the traditional dum cooking method, Krishnagiri biryani has a unique flavour that sets it apart from any other biryani in South India!

Read our complete Krishnagiri Biryani guide!

3. Krishnagiri is Famous for Hogenakal Falls 🌊

Krishnagiri district is home to the famous Hogenakal Falls — known as the Niagara of India! This stunning waterfall on the Kaveri river attracts thousands of tourists every year and is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Tamil Nadu!

4. Krishnagiri is Famous for its Fort 🏰

The historic Krishnagiri Fort sitting on top of a hill is one of the most famous landmarks in the district! Built centuries ago, this fort offers breathtaking panoramic views of the entire district and has great historical significance!

5. Krishnagiri is Famous for Halwa 🍩

Krishnagiri Halwa is one of the most famous sweets in Tamil Nadu! Made from wheat flour, sugar and ghee, this rich and delicious sweet is a must try for anyone visiting Krishnagiri. Available at PDS Jamuna Sweets and most sweet shops in the town!

6. Krishnagiri is Famous for Street Food 🌶️

Krishnagiri has an amazing street food scene! From sundal and sweet corn to pani puri and bajji, the streets of Krishnagiri come alive in the evenings with delicious local snacks and food!

7. Krishnagiri is Famous for Mango Festival 🥭🎉

Every year Krishnagiri hosts the famous Krishnagiri Mango Festival during peak mango season! This festival celebrates the rich mango heritage of Krishnagiri with exhibitions, tastings, competitions and amazing street food!

Read our complete Krishnagiri Mango Festival guide!

8. Krishnagiri is Famous for KRP Dam 💧

The KRP Dam Park in Krishnagiri district is a very popular tourist attraction! With beautiful sunset views, peaceful surroundings and fresh fish available from local vendors, the dam is a perfect destination for families and nature lovers!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Krishnagiri most famous for?

Krishnagiri is most famous for its mangoes! Known as the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu, Krishnagiri produces the finest mangoes in the state. It is also famous for its unique biryani, Hogenakal Falls and historic fort!

2. What food is Krishnagiri famous for?

Krishnagiri is famous for biryani, fresh mangoes, halwa, sundal, sweet corn, bajji and pani puri. The district has a rich and diverse food culture that makes it a must visit destination for food lovers!

3. Why is Krishnagiri called Mango Capital?

Krishnagiri is called the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu because it produces more mangoes than any other district in the state! The fertile soil and perfect climate of Krishnagiri make its mangoes exceptionally sweet and delicious!

4. What are the famous tourist places in Krishnagiri?

The most famous tourist places in Krishnagiri are Krishnagiri Fort, Krishnagiri Dam, Hogenakal Falls, Rayakottai Fort, KRP Dam Park and the beautiful Mango Orchards!
