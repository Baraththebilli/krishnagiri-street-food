Nearest Railway Station to Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri town does not have its own railway station. The nearest railway stations to Krishnagiri are Hosur and Dharmapuri. Here is your complete guide to reaching Krishnagiri by train!

1. Hosur Railway Station — 40 km from Krishnagiri 🚂

The nearest railway station to Krishnagiri is Hosur Railway Station, located approximately 40 km away! Hosur is well connected by train to Bangalore, Chennai and other major cities. From Hosur you can take a bus or taxi to Krishnagiri town easily!

📍 Hosur Railway Station, Hosur, Tamil Nadu

🚌 From Hosur to Krishnagiri: 45 minutes to 1 hour by bus or taxi

💰 Bus fare: ₹30 to ₹50 | Taxi fare: ₹400 to ₹600

🚂 Trains from Bangalore to Hosur: Multiple trains daily

2. Dharmapuri Railway Station — 45 km from Krishnagiri 🚂

Dharmapuri Railway Station is another nearby station approximately 45 km from Krishnagiri! Dharmapuri is connected by train to Chennai, Salem and other cities in Tamil Nadu. From Dharmapuri you can take a bus or taxi to Krishnagiri!

📍 Dharmapuri Railway Station, Dharmapuri, Tamil Nadu

🚌 From Dharmapuri to Krishnagiri: 1 hour by bus or taxi

💰 Bus fare: ₹40 to ₹60 | Taxi fare: ₹500 to ₹700

3. Bangalore Railway Station — 90 km from Krishnagiri 🚂

For travelers coming from North India or other states, Bangalore is the most convenient major railway hub near Krishnagiri! Bangalore is just 90 km from Krishnagiri and has excellent train connectivity to all major Indian cities!

📍 KSR Bangalore City Railway Station, Bangalore

🚌 From Bangalore to Krishnagiri: 2 hours by bus or taxi via NH44

💰 Bus fare: ₹100 to ₹200 | Taxi fare: ₹1500 to ₹2500

🚂 Trains from all major cities to Bangalore: Available daily

How to Reach Krishnagiri from Nearest Railway Stations

From Hosur Railway Station:

  • 🚌 Take a direct bus from Hosur Bus Stand to Krishnagiri
  • 🚕 Take a taxi or auto rickshaw from Hosur station
  • ⏰ Travel time: 45 minutes to 1 hour

From Dharmapuri Railway Station:

  • 🚌 Take a direct bus from Dharmapuri to Krishnagiri
  • 🚕 Take a taxi from Dharmapuri station
  • ⏰ Travel time: 1 hour

From Bangalore Railway Station:

  • 🚌 Take a direct KSRTC or Tamil Nadu bus to Krishnagiri
  • 🚕 Take a taxi via NH44 highway
  • ⏰ Travel time: 2 hours

Other Ways to Reach Krishnagiri

  • 🚌 **By Bus** — Best option! Direct buses from Chennai, Bangalore, Salem and Coimbatore
  • 🚗 **By Car** — Via NH44 from Bangalore or Chennai
  • ✈️ **By Air** — Nearest airports are Bangalore (90 km) and Chennai (260 km)

Frequently Asked Questions

No, Krishnagiri town does not have its own railway station. The nearest railway stations are Hosur (40 km) and Dharmapuri (45 km)!

2. What is the nearest station to Krishnagiri?

The nearest railway station to Krishnagiri is Hosur Railway Station, located approximately 40 km away. From Hosur you can take a bus or taxi to Krishnagiri in about 45 minutes!

3. How to reach Krishnagiri from Bangalore by train?

Take a train from Bangalore to Hosur Railway Station. From Hosur take a bus or taxi to Krishnagiri. Total journey time is approximately 2 to 3 hours!
