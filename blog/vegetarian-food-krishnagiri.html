Vegetarian Food in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri has a wonderful variety of vegetarian food options! From traditional South Indian breakfast to delicious street food snacks, vegetarians will absolutely love the food scene in Krishnagiri!

Best Vegetarian Street Foods in Krishnagiri

  • 🍢 Sundal — Boiled chickpeas with coconut and spices
  • 🌽 Roasted Corn — Perfect evening snack
  • 🥮 Bajji — Crispy fried vegetables in spiced batter
  • 🍩 Halwa — Famous Krishnagiri sweet
  • 🥭 Mango Snacks — Fresh and delicious during mango season
  • 🧆 Vada — Crispy and hot with coconut chutney
  • 🥞 Idiyappam — Soft string hoppers with coconut milk

Best Vegetarian Restaurants in Krishnagiri

Hotel Saravana Bhavan

The most popular vegetarian restaurant in Krishnagiri! Famous for their idli, dosa, and South Indian thali meals. A must visit for vegetarians in Krishnagiri!

Murugan Idli Shop

Famous across Tamil Nadu for their soft and fluffy idlis served with amazing chutneys and sambar. A vegetarian paradise in Krishnagiri!

Sri Annapoorna Restaurant

Known for their authentic Tamil Nadu vegetarian cuisine. Their unlimited thali meal is the best value for money vegetarian option in Krishnagiri!

Best Vegetarian Breakfast in Krishnagiri

  • 🫓 Dosa with coconut chutney
  • 🧆 Vada with sambar
  • 🍜 Pongal with ghee

Vegetarian Travel Tips for Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Most local restaurants offer pure vegetarian options
  • ✅ Look for restaurants with "Veg" sign outside
  • ✅ Morning is the best time for fresh breakfast items
  • ✅ Street food stalls offer many vegetarian options
  • ✅ During mango season fresh fruit is available everywhere
