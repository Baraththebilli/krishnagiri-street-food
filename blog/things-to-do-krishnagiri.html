Top Things to Do in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is a wonderful destination in Tamil Nadu with so many exciting activities for tourists and locals alike! Here is your complete guide to the best things to do in Krishnagiri!

1. Visit Krishnagiri Dam

One of the most popular things to do in Krishnagiri is visiting the beautiful Krishnagiri Dam. Enjoy boating, photography, and stunning sunset views at this peaceful location!

2. Explore Krishnagiri Fort

Trek up to the historic Krishnagiri Fort and enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the entire district. A must do activity for history lovers and adventure seekers!

3. Visit Hogenakal Falls

Take a day trip to the magnificent Hogenakal Falls near Krishnagiri. Enjoy boat rides through the rocky gorges and experience the natural beauty of this stunning waterfall!

4. Explore Mango Orchards

During mango season visit the beautiful mango orchards around Krishnagiri. Experience fresh mango picking and taste the most delicious mangoes straight from the tree!

5. Street Food Tour

Go on a street food tour of Krishnagiri and taste all the amazing local food! From biryani to sundal to fresh mango snacks — Krishnagiri street food is an experience you will never forget! Check out our Top 10 Street Foods guide!

6. Shopping at Local Markets

Explore the vibrant local markets of Krishnagiri and shop for fresh mangoes, local spices, handicrafts, and traditional Tamil Nadu products at very affordable prices!

7. Photography at Scenic Spots

Krishnagiri has many beautiful scenic spots perfect for photography! From the dam to the fort to the mango orchards — every corner of Krishnagiri offers a perfect photo opportunity!

Best Time to Visit Krishnagiri

The best time to visit Krishnagiri is between October and March when the weather is cool and pleasant. For mango lovers the best time is April to July during the famous mango season!
