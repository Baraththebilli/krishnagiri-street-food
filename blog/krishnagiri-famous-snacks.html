Famous Snacks in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is a paradise for snack lovers! The district has a unique variety of local snacks that are famous across Tamil Nadu. Whether you are a local or a tourist visiting Krishnagiri for the first time, these famous snacks are a must try!

1. Sundal 🍢

Sundal is the most famous snack in Krishnagiri! Made from boiled chickpeas seasoned with fresh coconut, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chillies, sundal is a healthy and delicious snack available at street stalls across Krishnagiri. Best enjoyed in the evenings!

✅ Available at most street food stalls in Krishnagiri

💰 Price: ₹10 to ₹30 per serving

2. Sweet Corn 🌽

Sweet corn is one of the most popular evening snacks in Krishnagiri! You can find sweet corn stalls all across Krishnagiri town serving hot and delicious sweet corn with butter and spices!

✅ Available near Krishnagiri Dam and main roads

💰 Price: ₹20 to ₹40 per cob

3. Bajji 🥮

Hot and crispy bajji is a monsoon favourite in Krishnagiri! Made from banana, potato, onion, or raw mango dipped in spiced batter and deep fried to perfection, bajji is the perfect snack to enjoy with a cup of hot tea during rainy evenings!

✅ Available at most tea shops and street stalls

💰 Price: ₹5 to ₹15 per piece

4. Mango Snacks 🥭

During mango season Krishnagiri streets are filled with amazing mango snacks! From raw mango chaat to sweet ripe mango slices to mango juice — the mango snack scene in Krishnagiri during April to July is absolutely incredible!

✅ Available during April to July mango season

💰 Price: ₹20 to ₹100 depending on variety

5. Murukku 🍢

Murukku is a traditional Tamil Nadu snack that is very popular in Krishnagiri! These crunchy spiral snacks made from rice flour are available at most sweet shops and street stalls in Krishnagiri. Perfect for snacking any time of the day!

✅ Available at sweet shops across Krishnagiri

💰 Price: ₹10 to ₹50 per pack

6. Pani Puri 🌶️

Pani Puri is extremely popular in Krishnagiri especially among young people and families! Crispy puris filled with spicy tangy water, chickpeas, and chutneys make it an unforgettable snack experience in Krishnagiri!

✅ Available near schools, colleges, and main market areas

💰 Price: ₹30 to ₹60 per plate

7. Krishnagiri Halwa 🍩

Krishnagiri Halwa is a famous sweet snack that the district is known for! Made from wheat, sugar, and ghee, this rich and delicious sweet is a must try for anyone visiting Krishnagiri. Available at most sweet shops in Krishnagiri town!

✅ Available at sweet shops in Krishnagiri town

💰 Price: ₹50 to ₹200 per kg

Best Areas for Snacks in Krishnagiri

  • 📍 Main Bus Stand area — highest variety of snacks
  • 📍 Old Market Road — traditional snack shops
  • 📍 Near Krishnagiri Dam — evening snack stalls
  • 📍 Salem Bypass Road — modern cafes and snack shops
