Day Trips from Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is perfectly located for amazing day trips! Surrounded by beautiful destinations in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka, you can easily visit many exciting places from Krishnagiri in just one day! Here are the best day trips from Krishnagiri!

1. Hogenakal Falls — 80 km from Krishnagiri

The most popular day trip from Krishnagiri! Known as the Niagara of India, Hogenakal Falls is a stunning waterfall on the Kaveri river. Enjoy boat rides through the rocky gorges and experience the breathtaking natural beauty! Best visited between June and October after the monsoon rains!

🚗 Distance: 80 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: June to October

2. Bangalore — 90 km from Krishnagiri

The Silicon Valley of India is just 90 km from Krishnagiri! Bangalore offers world class shopping, amazing restaurants, beautiful parks like Lalbagh and Cubbon Park, and exciting entertainment options. A perfect day trip for shopping and city experiences!

🚗 Distance: 90 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: Any time of the year

3. Hosur — 40 km from Krishnagiri

The rapidly growing industrial city of Hosur is just 40 km from Krishnagiri! Known as the Detroit of India, Hosur offers great shopping, food, and modern entertainment. A quick and easy day trip from Krishnagiri!

🚗 Distance: 40 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: Any time of the year

4. Salem — 90 km from Krishnagiri

The Steel City of Tamil Nadu is just 90 km from Krishnagiri! Salem offers beautiful temples, amazing local food including the famous Salem Thattu Vada, Yercaud hill station nearby, and great shopping. A wonderful day trip from Krishnagiri!

🚗 Distance: 90 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: October to February

5. Dharmapuri — 40 km from Krishnagiri

The neighboring district of Dharmapuri is just 40 km from Krishnagiri! Known for its beautiful Hogenakkal Falls, ancient temples, and local food, Dharmapuri makes for a great day trip from Krishnagiri!

🚗 Distance: 40 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: Any time of the year

6. Yercaud — 120 km from Krishnagiri

The Poor Man's Ooty! Yercaud is a beautiful hill station near Salem just 120 km from Krishnagiri. Famous for its coffee and orange plantations, beautiful lake, and cool weather, Yercaud is a perfect day trip for nature lovers from Krishnagiri!

🚗 Distance: 120 km from Krishnagiri

⏰ Best time: October to February

Travel Tips for Day Trips from Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Start early morning for longer day trips
  • ✅ Carry water and snacks for the journey
  • ✅ Check road conditions before travelling
  • ✅ NH44 connects Krishnagiri to most destinations
  • ✅ Buses are available from Krishnagiri bus stand to all destinations
