About Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is a beautiful district in Tamil Nadu located about 90 km from Bangalore and 260 km from Chennai. Known as the mango capital of Tamil Nadu, Krishnagiri is a wonderful destination for food lovers, nature enthusiasts, and history buffs!

How to Reach Krishnagiri

By Bus 🚌

Krishnagiri is well connected by bus from Chennai, Bangalore, Coimbatore, Salem, and all major cities in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. The main bus stand is located in the heart of Krishnagiri town!

By Train 🚂

The nearest railway station to Krishnagiri is Hosur railway station which is about 40 km away. From there you can take a bus or taxi to Krishnagiri town easily!

By Car 🚗

Krishnagiri is easily accessible by car via NH44 from Bangalore and NH48 from Chennai. The roads are well maintained and the drive through the scenic Tamil Nadu countryside is very enjoyable!

Getting Around Krishnagiri

  • 🛺 Auto rickshaws — Most convenient for short distances
  • 🚌 Local buses — Cheapest way to get around
  • 🏍️ Bike rentals — Best for exploring at your own pace
  • 🚗 Taxis — Available for day trips to nearby attractions

Travel Tips for Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Carry cash as many local shops don't accept cards
  • ✅ Try the local street food — it's safe and delicious!
  • ✅ Visit during October to February for best weather
  • ✅ Book accommodation in advance during mango festival season
  • ✅ Learn a few basic Tamil words — locals appreciate it!
