Best Non Veg Restaurants in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is a paradise for non vegetarian food lovers! From the famous Krishnagiri biryani to mutton nalli pepper fry and chicken dishes, the non veg restaurants in Krishnagiri serve some of the most delicious food in Tamil Nadu! Here is your complete guide to the best non veg restaurants in Krishnagiri!

1. Noor Hotel ⭐ 4.2

One of the most loved non veg restaurants in Krishnagiri! Noor Hotel is famous for its amazing chicken biryani and value for money non veg dishes. Located on Stadium Road, this is a must visit for non veg food lovers in Krishnagiri!

📍 Stadium Road, Wahab Nagar, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 95974 23290

🍽️ Famous for: Chicken Biryani, Chicken Curry

2. Aasife Biryani ⭐ 4.2

A very popular non veg restaurant in Krishnagiri known for its aromatic biryani with perfectly balanced spices and tender meat! Located near Rayakottai Flyover, Aasife Biryani is a favourite among locals and tourists alike!

📍 Near Rayakottai Flyover, Anna Arch, Somarpettai, Londenpet, Krishnagiri

⏰ 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily

🍽️ Famous for: Mutton Biryani, Chicken Biryani

3. Hotel Mangalam Non Veg A/C ⭐ 4.0

One of the most popular non veg restaurants in Krishnagiri with over 7000 reviews! Hotel Mangalam is famous for its outstanding mutton biryani made with seeraga samba rice and the legendary mutton nalli pepper fry!

📍 Near Fly Over, Rayakotta Road, Londenpet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 73390 97543

⏰ 8:30 AM to 10:30 PM daily

🍽️ Famous for: Mutton Biryani, Mutton Nalli Pepper Fry

4. Sibaa Restaurants ⭐ 4.0

A hidden gem for non veg food lovers in Krishnagiri! Sibaa Restaurants is famous for its mutton biryani with generous portions of tender and juicy mutton pieces. A must visit for biryani lovers in Krishnagiri!

📍 Salem Main Road, New Pet, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 94435 29696

⏰ 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily

🍽️ Famous for: Mutton Biryani, Mutton Curry

5. Hunger Brunch ⭐ 4.7

One of the highest rated restaurants in Krishnagiri! Hunger Brunch is famous for its delicious shawarma, chicken chilli momos and sandwiches. Perfect for a quick and affordable non veg meal in Krishnagiri!

📍 Salem Bypass Road, Thiruvalluvar Nagar, Krishnagiri

📞 +91 82488 50208

⏰ 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM daily

🍽️ Famous for: Shawarma, Chicken Momos, Sandwiches

6. Salem RR Biryani ⭐ 3.7

Located on the Chennai-Bangalore Bypass near Krishnagiri, Salem RR Biryani is a popular highway restaurant famous for its Chicken Bucket Biryani and Tandoori Chicken. Perfect for travelers passing through Krishnagiri!

📍 Chennai-Bangalore Bypass, Bayanapalli, Tamil Nadu

⏰ 10:00 AM to 10:30 PM daily

🍽️ Famous for: Chicken Bucket Biryani, Tandoori Chicken

Tips for Non Veg Dining in Krishnagiri

  • ✅ Visit during lunch 12pm to 3pm for freshest biryani
  • ✅ Popular shops sell out quickly — arrive early!
  • ✅ Try mutton biryani for the most authentic Krishnagiri experience
  • ✅ Ask for extra raita and salna on the side
  • ✅ Most non veg restaurants are affordable — expect ₹80 to ₹250 per plate

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best non veg restaurant in Krishnagiri?

The best non veg restaurants in Krishnagiri are Noor Hotel, Aasife Biryani and Hotel Mangalam! All three are highly rated and serve authentic Krishnagiri style non veg food!

2. What is the best non veg food in Krishnagiri?

The best non veg food in Krishnagiri is the mutton biryani at Hotel Mangalam and Aasife Biryani, the chicken biryani at Noor Hotel, and the mutton nalli pepper fry at Hotel Mangalam!

3. Are non veg restaurants affordable in Krishnagiri?

Yes! Non veg food in Krishnagiri is very affordable. Most restaurants charge between ₹80 to ₹250 per plate making it great value for money!
