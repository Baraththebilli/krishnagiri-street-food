Famous Food Items in Krishnagiri

Krishnagiri is home to some of the most delicious and unique food items in Tamil Nadu! From the famous Krishnagiri biryani to fresh mango snacks, here is your complete list of must try famous food items in Krishnagiri!

1. Krishnagiri Biryani 🍚

The most famous food item in Krishnagiri! Krishnagiri biryani is made with seeraga samba rice, special local spices and tender meat using the traditional dum cooking method. Available at Noor Hotel, Aasife Biryani and Hotel Mangalam!

💰 Price: ₹80 to ₹200 per plate

⭐ Must try: Mutton biryani with salna and raita!

2. Krishnagiri Mangoes 🥭

Krishnagiri is the Mango Capital of Tamil Nadu! The most famous mango food items include fresh mango slices, mango juice, mango chaat and mango halwa. Available during April to July mango season!

💰 Price: ₹20 to ₹100 depending on variety

⭐ Must try: Fresh Alphonso mango slices!

3. Krishnagiri Halwa 🍩

The most famous sweet food item in Krishnagiri! Made from wheat flour, sugar and ghee, Krishnagiri halwa has a unique texture and rich flavour. Available at PDS Jamuna Sweets and most sweet shops in Krishnagiri town!

💰 Price: ₹150 to ₹300 per kg

⭐ Must try: Fresh halwa from PDS Jamuna!

4. Sundal 🍢

A famous healthy street food item in Krishnagiri! Made from boiled chickpeas seasoned with fresh coconut, mustard seeds and curry leaves. Available at most street food stalls in the evenings!

💰 Price: ₹10 to ₹30 per serving

⭐ Best enjoyed as an evening snack!

5. Sweet Corn 🌽

Sweet corn is one of the most popular food items in Krishnagiri! Available at stalls all across the town, sweet corn served with butter and spices is a perfect evening snack for everyone!

💰 Price: ₹30 to ₹60 per serving

⭐ Best enjoyed in the evening near Krishnagiri Dam!

6. Bajji 🥮

Hot and crispy bajji is a very popular food item in Krishnagiri! Made from banana, potato or onion dipped in spiced batter and deep fried. Best enjoyed with a cup of hot tea during evenings!

💰 Price: ₹5 to ₹15 per piece

⭐ Best during monsoon evenings!

7. Pani Puri 🌶️

Pani Puri is an extremely popular food item in Krishnagiri especially among young people! Crispy puris filled with spicy tangy water, chickpeas and chutneys. Available near schools, colleges and main market areas!

💰 Price: ₹30 to ₹60 per plate

⭐ Try the extra spicy version for the full experience!

8. Mutton Nalli Pepper Fry 🍖

A special food item unique to Krishnagiri! The mutton nalli pepper fry at Hotel Mangalam is famous across the district for its rich spicy flavour and tender meat. A must try for non vegetarians visiting Krishnagiri!

💰 Price: ₹150 to ₹250 per plate

⭐ Available at Hotel Mangalam on Rayakotta Road!

9. Idli and Dosa 🥞

Soft fluffy idli and crispy dosa are famous breakfast food items in Krishnagiri! Available at most restaurants and tea shops from early morning. Best enjoyed with coconut chutney and sambar!

💰 Price: ₹30 to ₹80 per plate

⭐ Best enjoyed at Sree Saravana Bhavan!

10. Palkova 🥛

Palkova is a famous traditional milk sweet food item in Krishnagiri! Made from slowly reduced milk and sugar, palkova from PDS Jamuna is particularly famous and makes a perfect gift to take home!

💰 Price: ₹200 to ₹350 per kg

⭐ Available at PDS Jamuna Sweets on Rayakottai Road!
